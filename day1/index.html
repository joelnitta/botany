Why plants? Why botany?

  • Discuss with your neighbor why plants matter

Why plants?

  • Food to eat
  • Fiber for clothing
  • Building materials for houses
  • Paper for books
  • Drugs for medicines
  • Oxygen to breath
  • Basis of the food chain
  • Mental well-being

  • Do you notice the plants around you? Why or why not?

Plant Awareness Disparity

  • (sometimes called “plant blindness”, but this term is not preferred because it is ableist)

  • The condition of not being aware of the plants around you, despite their importance

  • This has real-life consequences!

    • Conservation bias: more money goes to conserving animals
    • Education bias: biology focuses mainly on animals

Why botany?

  • Botany helps us organize plant diversity and understand how plants evolved (and are evolving)

Goals of this course

  • To cure you of plant awareness disparity!

By the end of the course, you should be able to:

  • Understand the basic plant body plan and composition of a plant cell
  • Explain the defining characteristics of the major lineages of land plants and how they are related to each other
  • Explain how plants are distributed on earth to form ecosystems and biomes

AI Policy

  • AI is allowed on a limited basis to assist you with the coursework, not to do it for you

  • For example, after writing the first draft of the weekly assignment on your own, you may use AI to help correct your English grammar and spelling

  • Usage of AI to generate the main content of any assignment is prohibited and will receive zero points

  • Bottom line: you are spending time and money to learn. Letting AI do everything is cheating yourself.
